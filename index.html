FAQ

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

Is IKS MinT optimized across all devices?

IKS MinT is optimized and responsive across all web browsers and devices, and it supports PWA as well. In addition, you can download and use this web app on any browser or mobile device. Our developers are now working to bring MinT to the Play Store as an android app soon, so stay sharp!


Can I book the Section services through email instead of IKS MinT?

You can book the Section services through mail till May 30, but requests via mail will be given a lower priority than those received through the app. From June 1st, the Section will be accepting requests submitted through IKS MinT; any request via mail will be rejected.

Can I cancel a service I’ve booked through IKS MinT?

You can cancel any service you have booked, any day before the event date. You’ll be able to do so even after the Section Execom has approved and processed your service request.


Does IKS MinT provide the history of bookings done by my account?

An account created by a user in IKS MinT is valid for a lifetime. You can access the details of all the bookings you’ve done and the information you’ve stored anytime, no matter how old it is. Admins of an OU can access the entire history of bookings done from the OU.
